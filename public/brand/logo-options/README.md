# NEO THE AGENCY Logo Options

This folder contains 10 SVG logo explorations for NEO THE AGENCY.

Files:

- `neo-logo-01-velocity.svg` - Closest to the current mark. Fast, bold, loud.
- `neo-logo-02-orbit.svg` - More polished and tech-driven, with an SEO growth signal.
- `neo-logo-03-stacked-badge.svg` - Useful for avatars, favicons, pitch decks and small spaces.
- `neo-logo-04-monogram-lockup.svg` - Simple, serious and easy to use across business documents.
- `neo-logo-05-signal.svg` - Best when the logo needs to explain growth at first glance.
- `neo-logo-06-framed.svg` - Feels structured, digital and premium without being too playful.
- `neo-logo-07-pulse.svg` - Good for a performance marketing story: leads, tracking and motion.
- `neo-logo-08-prism.svg` - Most modern and app-like, useful if the brand should feel newer.
- `neo-logo-09-apex.svg` - More luxury and founder-led. Good for high-ticket clients.
- `neo-logo-10-grid-system.svg` - Strong for a studio that builds repeatable marketing machines.

Open `index.html` to compare every option on one preview sheet.
